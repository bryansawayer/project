Our Team

Sophia Patel – Innovation and Sustainability Officer Sophia Patel is the creative force behind DeepBlue Dynamics’ cutting-edge technologies and sustainable product designs. With a degree in environmental engineering and a passion for innovation, Sophia has developed eco-friendly solutions that merge science and practicality, such as the ReefGuard Artificial Reef Modules and AquaClear Water Filtration Systems

Mike Carter – Director of Community Engagement Mike Carter brings a dynamic approach to connecting people with the mission of DeepBlue Dynamics. With a background in environmental education and grassroots organizing, he has spent the last decade empowering coastal communities to take an active role in marine conservation.

Dr. Elena Marquez – Chief Marine Scientist  With over 15 years of experience in marine biology and ecological restoration, Dr. Elena Marquez leads DeepBlue Dynamics’ scientific initiatives. A renowned expert in coral reef ecosystems, Elena has spearheaded groundbreaking projects that have restored over 200 acres of reefs worldwide.
